<p>The <b>Franconian Rezat</b> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <span lang="de" xml:lang="de"><i>Fränkische Rezat</i></span>) is a 63,5&#160;km long <a href="/wiki/River" title="River">river</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. It is the western, left source river of the <a href="/wiki/Rednitz" title="Rednitz">Rednitz</a>. It rises in the <a href="/wiki/Franconian_Heights" title="Franconian Heights">Franconian Heights</a> near <a href="/wiki/Oberdachstetten" title="Oberdachstetten">Oberdachstetten</a>. It flows generally east through the towns <a href="/wiki/Lehrberg" title="Lehrberg">Lehrberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ansbach" title="Ansbach">Ansbach</a>, <a href="/wiki/Windsbach" title="Windsbach">Windsbach</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spalt" title="Spalt">Spalt</a>. Together with the Swabian Rezat (<i>Schwäbische Rezat</i>), it forms the Rednitz in <a href="/wiki/Georgensgm%C3%BCnd" title="Georgensgmünd">Georgensgmünd</a>.</p>
