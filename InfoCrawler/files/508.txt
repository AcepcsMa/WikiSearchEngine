<p><b>Spielwarenmesse</b> is the largest international <a href="/wiki/Trade_fair" title="Trade fair">trade fair</a> for <a href="/wiki/Toy" title="Toy">toys</a> and <a href="/wiki/Game" title="Game">games</a> hold annually since 1949.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Only trade visitors associated with the toy business, journalists and invited guests are admitted. Each year during the course of the event which is held for six days, about 2,800 exhibitors from about 60 countries present their products.<sup id="cite_ref-brochure2018_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-brochure2018-1">[1]</a></sup> In 2017, 73,000 trade visitors and purchasers from 123 countries came for the fair.<sup id="cite_ref-brochure2018_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-brochure2018-1">[1]</a></sup> The fair is organized by <a href="/wiki/Spielwarenmesse_eG" title="Spielwarenmesse eG">Spielwarenmesse eG</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> a marketing and trade fair service provider, based in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Range_of_products">Range of products</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nuremberg_International_Toy_Fair&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Range of products">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Every year, about one million products are displayed on the fair, including approximately 70,000 new products. On the fair, they are presented in twelve different product groups. These are, as of the 2011 fair:</p>
<ul>
<li>Model Construction, <a href="/wiki/Hobbies" class="mw-redirect" title="Hobbies">Hobbies</a></li>
<li>Model Railways and Accessories</li>
<li>Technical Toys, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Educational_Toys&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Educational Toys (page does not exist)">Educational Toys</a>, Action Toys</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Doll" title="Doll">Dolls</a>, Soft Toys,</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Game" title="Game">Games</a>, Books, Learning and Experimenting, <a href="/wiki/Multimedia" title="Multimedia">Multimedia</a></li>
<li>Festive and Trend Articles, Carnival</li>
<li><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.woodentoyshop.co.uk">Wooden Toys</a>, Craftworks, Gifts</li>
<li>Arts&amp; Crafts, Creative Design</li>
<li>Sports, Leisure, Outdoor</li>
<li>School Articles, Stationery</li>
<li>Baby &amp; Infant Articles</li>
<li>Multi-product Group</li>
</ul>
<h2><span id="ToyInnovation/ToyAward"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="ToyInnovation.2FToyAward">ToyInnovation/ToyAward</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nuremberg_International_Toy_Fair&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: ToyInnovation/ToyAward">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The awarded products stand out due to their degree of innovation, product concept, creativity and playing idea. A jury of industry experts determines the winners in five categories (as of Spielwarenmesse International Toy Fair 2011):<sup id="cite_ref-ToyAward_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ToyAward-4">[4]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Baby&amp;Infant</li>
<li>PreSchool</li>
<li>SchoolKids</li>
<li>Teenager&amp;Family</li>
<li>SpecialAward (Topic changing annually)</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Global_Toy_Conference">Global Toy Conference</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nuremberg_International_Toy_Fair&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Global Toy Conference">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Global Toy Conference takes place on the last day of the fair and deals with issues concerning the future of the toy trade and industry, e.g. sustainability, toy safety, online marketing and successful selling on the Internet.</p>
