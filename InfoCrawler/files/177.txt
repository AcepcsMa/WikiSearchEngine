<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Instructions_for_nominators">Instructions for nominators</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-1" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Create a subpage for your new DYK suggestion and then list the page below under the <b>date the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYKTN#article" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYKTN">article</a> was created or the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYKTN#expansion" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYKTN">expansion</a> began</b> (<u><b>not</b></u> the date you submit it here), with the newest dates at the bottom. Any registered user may <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYKTN#nominate" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYKTN">nominate</a> a DYK suggestion (if you are not a registered user, please leave a message at the bottom of <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:DYK" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia talk:DYK">the DYK project talk page</a> with the details of the article you would like to nominate and the hook you would like to propose); <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYKTN#self-nom" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYKTN">self-nominations</a> are permitted and encouraged. Thanks for participating and please remember to check back for comments on your nomination (consider <a href="/wiki/Help:Watchlist" title="Help:Watchlist">watchlisting</a> your nomination page).</p>
<center><i><b>If this is your first nomination, please read the DYK rules before continuing:</b></i></center>
<center><i>Official DYK criteria:</i> <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know#DYK_rules" title="Wikipedia:Did you know">DYK rules</a></b> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Supplementary_guidelines" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Supplementary guidelines">supplementary guidelines</a></center>
<center><i>Unofficial guide:</i> <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Learning_DYK" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Learning DYK">Learning DYK</a></center>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="To_nominate_an_article"><span id="How_to_post_a_new_nomination"></span>To nominate an article</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-2" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox" style="margin: auto; width:50%; padding: 10px; border: 0px solid #aaa; font-size: 88%; background-color: #E7E7E7;">

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="How_to_review_a_nomination">How to review a nomination</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-3" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Main page: <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Reviewing_guide" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Reviewing guide">Wikipedia:Did you know/Reviewing guide</a></div>
<p>Any editor who was not involved in writing/expanding or nominating an article may review it by checking to see that the article meets all the DYK criteria (<a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Article_length" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Article length">long enough</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/New_article" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/New article">new enough</a>, no serious <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know#contentrules" title="Wikipedia:Did you know">editorial or content issues</a>) and the hook is cited. Editors may also alter the suggested hook to improve it, suggest new hooks, or even lend a hand and make edits to the article to which the hook applies so that the hook is supported and accurate. For a more detailed discussion of the DYK rules and review process see the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Supplementary_guidelines" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Supplementary guidelines">supplementary guidelines</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Reviewing_guide" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Reviewing guide">WP:Did you know/Reviewing guide</a>.</p>
<p>To post a comment or review on a DYK nomination, follow the steps outlined below:</p>
<ul>
<li>Look through this page, <a class="mw-selflink selflink">Template talk:Did you know</a>, to find a nomination you would like to comment on.</li>
<li>Click the "Review or comment" link at the top of the nomination. You will be taken to the nomination subpage.</li>
<li>The top of the page includes a list of the DYK criteria. Check the article to ensure it meets all the relevant criteria.</li>
<li>To indicate the result of the review (i.e., whether the nomination passes, fails, or needs some minor changes), leave a signed comment on the page. Please begin with one of the 5 review symbols that appear at the top of the edit screen, and then indicate all aspects of the article that you have reviewed; your comment should look something like the following:
<blockquote>
<p><code>Article length and age are fine, no copyvio or plagiarism concerns, reliable sources are used. But the hook needs to be shortened.</code></p>
</blockquote>
If you are the first person to comment on the nomination, there will be a line <code>:* &lt;!-- REPLACE THIS LINE TO WRITE FIRST COMMENT, KEEPING :* --&gt;</code> showing you where you should put the comment.</li>
<li>Save the page.</li>
</ul>
<p>If there is any problem or concern about a nomination, please consider notifying the nominator by placing <b>{{subst:DYKproblem|Article|header=yes|sig=yes}}</b> on the nominator's talk page.</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Frequently_asked_questions">Frequently asked questions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-4" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span id="Backlogged?"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Backlogged.3F">Backlogged?</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-5" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>This page is often backlogged. As long as your submission is still on the page, it will stay there until an editor reviews it. Since editors are encouraged to review the oldest submissions first (so that those hooks don't grow stale), it may take several weeks until your submission is reviewed. In the meantime, please consider reviewing another submission (not your own) to help reduce the backlog (see instructions above).</p>
<h4><span id="Where_is_my_hook?"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Where_is_my_hook.3F">Where is my hook?</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-6" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>If you can't find the nomination you submitted to <a class="mw-selflink selflink">this nominations</a> page, it may have been approved and is on the <a href="/wiki/Template_talk:Did_you_know/Approved" title="Template talk:Did you know/Approved">approved nominations</a> page waiting to be promoted. It could also have been added to one of the <a href="/wiki/Template:Did_you_know/Queue#Prep_areas" title="Template:Did you know/Queue">prep areas</a>, promoted from prep to a <a href="/wiki/Template:Did_you_know/Queue#Queues" title="Template:Did you know/Queue">queue</a>, or is on the main page.</p>
<p>If the nominated hook is in none of those places, then the nomination has probably been rejected. Such a rejection usually only occurs if it was at least a couple of weeks old and had unresolved issues for which any discussion had gone stale. If you think your nomination was unfairly rejected, you can query this on the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:DYK" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia talk:DYK">DYK discussion page</a>, but as a general rule such nominations will only be restored in exceptional circumstances.</p>

<li>Hooks that have been approved are located on the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:DYK/A" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:DYK/A">approved nominations page</a>.</li>
<li>In one window, open the DYK nomination subpage of the hook you would like to promote.</li>
<li>In another window, open the <b><a href="/wiki/Template:Did_you_know/Queue#Prep_areas" title="Template:Did you know/Queue">prep set</a></b> you intend to add the hook to.</li>
<li>In the prep set...
<ul>
<li>Paste the hook into the hook area (be sure to not paste in <i>that that</i>)</li>
<li>Paste the credit information (<span class="nowrap">{{DYKmake}}</span> and/or <span class="nowrap">{{DYKnom}}</span>) into the credits area.</li>
<li>Add an edit summary, e.g. "Promoted [[Jane Fonda]]", preview, and save</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Back on DYK nomination page...
<ul>
<li>change <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code>{{DYKsubpage</code></span> to <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code>{{<b>subst:</b>DYKsubpage</code></span></li>
<li>change <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code class="nowrap">|passed=</code></span> to <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code class="nowrap">|passed=yes</code></span></li>
<li>Add an edit summary, e.g. "Promoted to Prep 3", preview, and save</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="How_to_remove_a_rejected_hook">How to remove a rejected hook</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-10" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Open the DYK nomination subpage of the hook you would like to remove. (It's best to wait several days after a reviewer has rejected the hook, just in case someone contests or the article undergoes a large change.)</li>
<li>In the window where the DYK nomination subpage is open, replace the line <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code>{{DYKsubpage</code></span> with <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code>{{<b>subst:</b>DYKsubpage</code></span>, and replace <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code class="nowrap">|passed=</code></span> with <span class="example good-example" style="font-family: Georgia, 'DejaVu Serif', serif; color: #006400;"><code class="nowrap">|passed=no</code></span>. Then save the page. This has the effect of wrapping up the discussion on the DYK nomination subpage in a blue archive box and stating that the nomination was unsuccessful, as well as adding the nomination to a category for archival purposes.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="How_to_remove_a_hook_from_the_prep_areas_or_queue">How to remove a hook from the prep areas or queue</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Did_you_know/Header&amp;action=edit&amp;section=T-11" title="Template talk:Did you know/Header">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Edit the prep area or queue where the hook is and remove the hook and the credits associated with it.</li>
<li>Go to the hook's nomination subpage (there should have been a link to it in the credits section).
<ul>
<li>View the edit history for that page</li>
<li>Go back to the last version <i>before</i> the edit where the hook was promoted, and revert to that version to make the nomination active again.</li>
<li>Add a new icon on the nomination subpage to cancel the previous tick and leave a comment after it explaining that the hook was removed from the prep area or queue, and why, so that later reviewers are aware of this issue.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Add a transclusion of the template back to this page so that reviewers can see it. It goes under the date that it was first created/expanded/listed as a GA. You may need to add back the day header for that date if it had been removed from this page.</li>
<li>If you removed the hook from a queue, it is best to either replace it with another hook from one of the prep areas, or to leave a message at <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia_talk:DYK" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia talk:DYK">WT:DYK</a> asking someone else to do so.</li>
<li>Add a link to the nomination subpage at <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Did_you_know/Removed" title="Wikipedia:Did you know/Removed">Wikipedia:Did you know/Removed</a> to help in tracking removals.</li>
</ul>
