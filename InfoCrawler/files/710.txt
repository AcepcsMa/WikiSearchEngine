				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Peter Bucher</b> (born April 3, 1947) is a former <a href="/wiki/West_Germany" title="West Germany">West German</a> <a href="/wiki/Team_handball" class="mw-redirect" title="Team handball">handball</a> player who competed in the <a href="/wiki/1972_Summer_Olympics" title="1972 Summer Olympics">1972 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-sr_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sr-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 1972 he was part of the West German team which finished sixth in the <a href="/wiki/Handball_at_the_1972_Summer_Olympics" title="Handball at the 1972 Summer Olympics">Olympic tournament</a>. He played all six matches and scored twenty goals.<sup id="cite_ref-sr_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sr-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
