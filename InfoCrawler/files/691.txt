<p><b>Kalkudah</b> or <b>Kalkuda</b> (Pronounced Kal-Kuda, <a href="/wiki/Tamil_language" title="Tamil language">Tamil</a> translation rock-bay) is a <a href="/wiki/Seaside_resort" title="Seaside resort">coastal resort town</a> located about 35 kilometers northwest of <a href="/wiki/Batticaloa" title="Batticaloa">Batticaloa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Batticaloa_District" title="Batticaloa District">Batticaloa District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a>. It used to be a popular tourist destination, however due to <a href="/wiki/2004_Indian_Ocean_earthquake" class="mw-redirect" title="2004 Indian Ocean earthquake">2004 Indian Ocean Tsunami</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lankan_Civil_War" title="Sri Lankan Civil War">Sri Lankan Civil War</a> tourist numbers have declined. <a href="/wiki/Pasikudah" title="Pasikudah">Pasikudah</a> and Kalkudah are located few kilometers apart.</p>
<p>In recent time, Kalkudah receives plenty of visitors to <a href="/wiki/Pasikudah" title="Pasikudah">Pasikudah</a> beach.</p>
