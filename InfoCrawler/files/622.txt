			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">U3 (Nuremberg U-Bahn)</h1>
									<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
									<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
								<div id="contentSub"></div>
												<div id="jump-to-nav" class="mw-jump">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
				</div>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>U3</b> is an underground line in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg_U-Bahn" title="Nuremberg U-Bahn">Nuremberg</a>. The line was opened on 14 June 2008. The stretch is about 5,3&#160;km long. The Number of stations is 9. The start-and endstations are Maxfeld and Gustav-Adolf-Straße. U 3 trains are run driverless. (automatic guideway transit abbr.:AGT)</p>
