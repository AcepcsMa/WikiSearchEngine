<p>The <b>Staatstheater Nürnberg</b> is a theatre company in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, Bavaria, Germany. The theatre is one of four Bavarian state theatres and shows operas, plays, ballets and concerts. Until 1 January 2005, it was the <i>Städtische Bühnen Nürnberg</i> (municipal stages)</p>
<p>Its main venue, the opera house (<i>Opernhaus Nürnberg</i>), is one of the largest theatres in German. It was built from 1903 to 1905 in <a href="/wiki/Art_Nouveau" title="Art Nouveau">Art Nouveau</a> style by the architect <a href="/wiki/Heinrich_Seeling" title="Heinrich Seeling">Heinrich Seeling</a>. Other venues are the play house (<i>Schauspielhaus Nürnberg</i>) including the small stages <i>Kammerspiele</i> and <i>BlueBox</i>, and the <a href="/w/index.php?title=Meistersingerhalle&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Meistersingerhalle (page does not exist)">Meistersingerhalle</a><span class="noprint" style="font-size:85%; font-style: normal;">&#160;(<a href="https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meistersingerhalle" class="extiw" title="de:Meistersingerhalle">de</a>)</span> where the concerts of the orchestra <a href="/w/index.php?title=Staatsphilharmonie_N%C3%BCrnberg&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Staatsphilharmonie Nürnberg (page does not exist)">Staatsphilharmonie Nürnberg</a><span class="noprint" style="font-size:85%; font-style: normal;">&#160;(<a href="https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Staatsphilharmonie_N%C3%BCrnberg" class="extiw" title="de:Staatsphilharmonie Nürnberg">de</a>)</span> are held. The <a href="/wiki/Generalmusikdirektor" class="mw-redirect" title="Generalmusikdirektor">Generalmusikdirektor</a> is <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Bosch" title="Marcus Bosch">Marcus Bosch</a>.</p>
