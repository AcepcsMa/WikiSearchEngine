<li><a href="/wiki/March_1" title="March 1">March 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/Mart%C3%ADn_Alonso_Pinz%C3%B3n" title="Martín Alonso Pinzón">Martín Alonso Pinzón</a> returns to the city of Bayona in Spain from the voyage of discovery, sending the first notice about the discovery to the Catholic Monarchs (<a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a> is delayed by a storm in the <a href="/wiki/Azores" title="Azores">Azores</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_15" title="March 15">March 15</a> – <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mart%C3%ADn_Alonso_Pinz%C3%B3n" title="Martín Alonso Pinzón">Martín Alonso Pinzón</a> return to <a href="/wiki/Palos_de_la_Frontera" title="Palos de la Frontera">Palos de la Frontera</a>, the original port in Spain from where they started the first voyage of discovery.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_4" title="May 4">May 4</a> – In the <a href="/wiki/Papal_bull" title="Papal bull">papal bull</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Inter_caetera" title="Inter caetera">Inter caetera</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VI" title="Pope Alexander VI">Pope Alexander VI</a> decrees that all lands discovered 100 leagues (or further west) of the <a href="/wiki/Azores" title="Azores">Azores</a> are Spanish.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_19" title="August 19">August 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Maximilian_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Maximilian I, Holy Roman Emperor">Maximilian I</a> succeeds his father, <a href="/wiki/Frederick_III,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick III, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick III</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Holy Roman Emperor">Holy Roman Emperor</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_9" title="September 9">September 9</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Krbava_Field" title="Battle of Krbava Field">Battle of Krbava Field</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">Croatia</a>: Forces of the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a> defeat those of the <a href="/wiki/Croatia_in_the_union_with_Hungary" class="mw-redirect" title="Croatia in the union with Hungary">Kingdom of Croatia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_26" title="September 26">September 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VI" title="Pope Alexander VI">Pope Alexander VI</a> issues the <a href="/wiki/Papal_bull" title="Papal bull">bull</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Dudum_siquidem" title="Dudum siquidem">Dudum siquidem</a></i> to the <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Monarchs" title="Catholic Monarchs">Catholic Monarchs</a>, extending the grant of newly discovered lands he made them in <i><a href="/wiki/Inter_caetera" title="Inter caetera">Inter caetera</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_29" title="September 29">September 29</a> – <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a> leaves <a href="/wiki/C%C3%A1diz" title="Cádiz">Cádiz</a> on his <a href="/wiki/Voyages_of_Christopher_Columbus#Second_voyage" title="Voyages of Christopher Columbus">second voyage of exploration</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_19" title="November 19">November 19</a> – Christopher Columbus lands on the coast of the island of Borinquen, which he renames <a href="/wiki/San_Juan,_Puerto_Rico" title="San Juan, Puerto Rico">San Juan</a> (modern-day <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>).</li>
</ul>
