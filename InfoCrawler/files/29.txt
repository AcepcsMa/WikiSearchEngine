<p><i><b>ACM Transactions on Information Systems</b></i>is a quarterly <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Scientific_journal" title="Scientific journal">scientific journal</a> covering research on <a href="/wiki/Computer_system" class="mw-redirect" title="Computer system">computer systems</a> and their underlying technology. It was established in 1983 and is published by the <a href="/wiki/Association_for_Computing_Machinery" title="Association for Computing Machinery">Association for Computing Machinery</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is <a href="/wiki/Maarten_de_Rijke" title="Maarten de Rijke">Maarten de Rijke</a> (<a href="/wiki/University_of_Amsterdam" title="University of Amsterdam">University of Amsterdam</a>). The journal is abstracted and indexed in the <a href="/wiki/Science_Citation_Index_Expanded" class="mw-redirect" title="Science Citation Index Expanded">Science Citation Index Expanded</a> and <a href="/wiki/Current_Contents" title="Current Contents">Current Contents</a>/Engineering, Computing &amp; Technology.<sup id="cite_ref-ISI_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ISI-1">[1]</a></sup> According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2012 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 1.070.<sup id="cite_ref-WoS_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WoS-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
