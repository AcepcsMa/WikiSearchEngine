<p><b>Changping</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans" xml:lang="zh-Hans">昌平乡</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant" xml:lang="zh-Hant">昌平鄉</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin" xml:lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Chāngpíng Xiāng</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Standard_Zhuang" title="Standard Zhuang">zhuang</a>: Canghbingz Yangh) is a Township under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Fusui_County" title="Fusui County">Fusui County</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/Guangxi_Zhuang_Autonomous_Region" class="mw-redirect" title="Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region">Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> As of 2011<sup class="plainlinks noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="//en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Changping_Township,_Fusui_County&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, it had an area of 196 square kilometres (76&#160;sq&#160;mi) populated by 25,054 people residing in 1 residential communities (社区) and 8 villages.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
