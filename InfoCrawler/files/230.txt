<p><b>September 25</b> is the 268th day of the year (269th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 97 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Friday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Wednesday or Thursday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Saturday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=September_25&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/275" title="275">275</a> – In Rome (after the assassination of <a href="/wiki/Aurelian" title="Aurelian">Aurelian</a>), the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Senate" title="Roman Senate">Senate</a> proclaims <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Claudius_Tacitus" class="mw-redirect" title="Marcus Claudius Tacitus">Marcus Claudius Tacitus</a> <a href="/wiki/Roman_emperor" title="Roman emperor">Emperor</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/762" title="762">762</a> – Led by <a href="/wiki/Muhammad_al-Nafs_al-Zakiyya" title="Muhammad al-Nafs al-Zakiyya">Muhammad al-Nafs al-Zakiyya</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hasan_ibn_Ali" title="Hasan ibn Ali">Hasanid</a> branch of the <a href="/wiki/Alids" title="Alids">Alids</a> begins the <a href="/wiki/Alid_Revolt_(762%E2%80%9363)" class="mw-redirect" title="Alid Revolt (762–63)">Alid Revolt</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Abbasid_Caliphate" title="Abbasid Caliphate">Abbasid Caliphate</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1066" title="1066">1066</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Stamford_Bridge" title="Battle of Stamford Bridge">Battle of Stamford Bridge</a> saw the defeat of <a href="/wiki/Harald_Hardrada" title="Harald Hardrada">Harald Hardrada</a> King of Norway by King <a href="/wiki/Harold_Godwinson" title="Harold Godwinson">Harold</a> II of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1237" title="1237">1237</a> – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scotland</a> sign the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_York" title="Treaty of York">Treaty of York</a>, establishing the location of their <a href="/wiki/Anglo-Scottish_border" title="Anglo-Scottish border">common border</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1396" title="1396">1396</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_dynasty" title="Ottoman dynasty">Ottoman Emperor</a> <a href="/wiki/Bayezid_I" title="Bayezid I">Bayezid I</a> defeats a Christian army at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Nicopolis" title="Battle of Nicopolis">Battle of Nicopolis</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1513" title="1513">1513</a> – Spanish explorer <a href="/wiki/Vasco_N%C3%BA%C3%B1ez_de_Balboa" title="Vasco Núñez de Balboa">Vasco Núñez de Balboa</a> reaches what would become known as the Pacific Ocean.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1555" title="1555">1555</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Peace_of_Augsburg" title="Peace of Augsburg">Peace of Augsburg</a> is signed in <a href="/wiki/Augsburg" title="Augsburg">Augsburg</a> by <a href="/wiki/Charles_V,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor">Charles V</a> and the princes of the <a href="/wiki/Schmalkaldic_League" title="Schmalkaldic League">Schmalkaldic League</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1690" title="1690">1690</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Publick_Occurrences_Both_Forreign_and_Domestick" title="Publick Occurrences Both Forreign and Domestick">Publick Occurrences Both Forreign and Domestick</a></i>, the first <a href="/wiki/Newspaper" title="Newspaper">newspaper</a> to appear in the Americas, is published for the first and only time.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1775" title="1775">1775</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Ethan_Allen" title="Ethan Allen">Ethan Allen</a> surrenders to British forces after attempting to capture <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Longue-Pointe" title="Battle of Longue-Pointe">Battle of Longue-Pointe</a>. <a href="/wiki/Benedict_Arnold" title="Benedict Arnold">Benedict Arnold</a> and <a href="/wiki/Benedict_Arnold%27s_expedition_to_Quebec" title="Benedict Arnold's expedition to Quebec">his expeditionary company</a> set off from <a href="/wiki/Fort_Western" title="Fort Western">Fort Western</a>, bound for <a href="/wiki/Quebec_City" title="Quebec City">Quebec City</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1789" title="1789">1789</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">United States Congress</a> passes twelve amendments to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">United States Constitution</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Congressional_Apportionment_Amendment" title="Congressional Apportionment Amendment">Congressional Apportionment Amendment</a> (which was never ratified), the <a href="/wiki/Twenty-seventh_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution" title="Twenty-seventh Amendment to the United States Constitution">Congressional Compensation Amendment</a>, and the ten that are known as the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Bill_of_Rights" title="United States Bill of Rights">Bill of Rights</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a> – <a href="/wiki/Peking_opera" title="Peking opera">Peking opera</a> is born when the Four Great Anhui Troupes introduce <a href="/wiki/Huiju" title="Huiju">Anhui opera</a> to Beijing in honor of the <a href="/wiki/Qianlong_Emperor" title="Qianlong Emperor">Qianlong Emperor</a>'s eightieth birthday.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Lakota_people" title="Lakota people">Teton Sioux</a> (a subdivision of the Lakota) demand one of the boats from the <a href="/wiki/Lewis_and_Clark_Expedition" title="Lewis and Clark Expedition">Lewis and Clark Expedition</a> as a toll for allowing the expedition to move further upriver.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1868" title="1868">1868</a> – The Imperial Russian steam frigate <i><a href="/wiki/Russian_frigate_Alexander_Nevsky" title="Russian frigate Alexander Nevsky">Alexander Nevsky</a></i> is shipwrecked off <a href="/wiki/Jutland" title="Jutland">Jutland</a> while carrying <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duke_Alexei_Alexandrovich_of_Russia" title="Grand Duke Alexei Alexandrovich of Russia">Grand Duke Alexei of Russia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1890" title="1890">1890</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">United States Congress</a> establishes <a href="/wiki/Sequoia_National_Park" title="Sequoia National Park">Sequoia National Park</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1906" title="1906">1906</a> – <a href="/wiki/Leonardo_Torres_y_Quevedo" title="Leonardo Torres y Quevedo">Leonardo Torres y Quevedo</a> demonstrates the <i>Telekino</i>, guiding a boat from the shore, in what is considered to be the first use of a <a href="/wiki/Remote_control" title="Remote control">remote control</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1911" title="1911">1911</a> – An explosion of badly degraded <a href="/wiki/Propellant" title="Propellant">propellant</a> charges on board the <a href="/wiki/French_battleship_Libert%C3%A9" title="French battleship Liberté">French battleship <i>Liberté</i></a> detonates the forward ammunition <a href="/wiki/Magazine_(artillery)" title="Magazine (artillery)">magazines</a> and destroys the ship.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1912" title="1912">1912</a> – <a href="/wiki/Columbia_University_Graduate_School_of_Journalism" title="Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism">Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism</a> is founded in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>.</li>
