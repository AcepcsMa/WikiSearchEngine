<p><b>Bundesautobahn&#160;73</b> (translates from <a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a> as <i>Federal Motorway&#160;73</i>, short form <b>Autobahn&#160;73</b>, abbreviated as <b>BAB&#160;73</b> or <b>A&#160;73</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Motorway" class="mw-redirect" title="Motorway">motorway</a> in Germany. It connects <a href="/wiki/Suhl" title="Suhl">Suhl</a> to <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>. The part between Bamberg and Nuremberg is also known as the <b>Frankenschnellweg</b>. Between exits Nürnberg/Fürth and Nürnberg-Hafen Ost it is not classified as Bundesautobahn. In Nürnberg-Gostenhof it is not an Autobahn and interrupted by crossings with traffic lights.</p>
