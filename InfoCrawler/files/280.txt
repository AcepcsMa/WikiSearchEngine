<p>Dr. <b>Ulrich Maly</b> (born 8 August 1960) is a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Politician" title="Politician">politician</a>, member of the <a href="/wiki/Social_Democratic_Party_of_Germany" title="Social Democratic Party of Germany">SPD</a> who has served as the <a href="/wiki/List_of_mayors_of_Nuremberg" title="List of mayors of Nuremberg">Mayor of Nuremberg</a> since 2002.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life">Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ulrich_Maly&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ulrich Maly was born in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavaria</a>, and educated at the elementary school in the Amberger Straße and the Johannes Scharrer <a href="/wiki/Gymnasium_(school)" title="Gymnasium (school)">Gymnasium</a>. Since 1967 he has been involved honorarily in the <i>Sozialistische Jugend Deutschlands – Die Falken</i> (<a href="/wiki/Socialist_Youth_of_Germany_%E2%80%93_Falcons" title="Socialist Youth of Germany – Falcons">Socialist Youth of Germany – Falcons</a>) and was delegated by them as the chairman of the Kreisjugendring (District Youth Association) Nuremberg-City. Since January 1984 Maly has been a member of the SPD. After the <a href="/wiki/Zivildienst" title="Zivildienst">Zivildienst</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Retirement_home" title="Retirement home">retirement home</a> at Mimberg near <a href="/wiki/Burgthann" title="Burgthann">Burgthann</a> he studied <a href="/wiki/Economics" title="Economics">economics</a> at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Erlangen-Nuremberg" title="University of Erlangen-Nuremberg">University of Erlangen-Nuremberg</a>, majoring in <a href="/wiki/Economic_policy" title="Economic policy">economic policy</a> and <a href="/wiki/Public_law" title="Public law">public law</a>.</p>
<p>In 1990 he received his <a href="/wiki/Doctorate#Country-specific_practice" title="Doctorate">Dr. rer. pol.</a> with a dissertation on "Economy and environment in municipal development policy." In the same year he became whip of the SPD <a href="/wiki/City_council" title="City council">city council</a> faction. From 1996 to 2002 Maly held the appointment of the city <a href="/wiki/Treasurer" title="Treasurer">treasurer</a> of Nuremberg. Since 1 May 2002 Ulrich Maly has been the <a href="/wiki/Lord_Mayor" class="mw-redirect" title="Lord Mayor">Lord Mayor</a> of the city of Nuremberg. In November 2005 he was elected to the SPD federal executive council.</p>
<p>He was shortlisted for the 2008 <a href="/wiki/World_Mayor" title="World Mayor">World Mayor</a> award.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tasks_and_functions">Tasks and functions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ulrich_Maly&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Tasks and functions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ulrich Maly attends, partially as the Lord Mayor of Nuremberg, a huge number of tasks and functions:</p>
<ul>
<li>Chairman of the board of several municipal subsidiaries, like the "Städtische Werke Nürnberg" (Municipal Plants Nuremberg), the "Wohnungsbaugesellschaft Nürnberg <a href="/wiki/GmbH" class="mw-redirect" title="GmbH">mbH</a>" (House Building Company Nuremberg) and the "NürnbergMesse" (Nuremberg Fair).</li>
<li>Member of the <a href="/wiki/Supervisory_board" title="Supervisory board">supervisory board</a> of the <a href="/wiki/1._FC_Nuremberg" class="mw-redirect" title="1. FC Nuremberg">1. FC Nuremberg</a>, the N-ERGIE and the <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg_Airport" title="Nuremberg Airport">Nuremberg Airport</a></li>
<li>Member in the board of the "Bayerischer Städtetag" and in the executive board of the "Deutscher Städtetag"</li>
<li>Member of the state board and the federal board of the SPD</li>
<li>Member of the <a href="/wiki/Committee_of_the_Regions" class="mw-redirect" title="Committee of the Regions">Committee of the Regions</a> in <a href="/wiki/Brussels" title="Brussels">Brussels</a> as a deputy of the "Deutscher Städtetag"</li>
<li>Chairman of the jury "Internationaler Nürnberger Menschenrechtspreis" (Nuremberg International Human Rights Award)</li>
<li>Foundation council chairman of the "Stiftung Staatstheater Nürnberg" (Foundation State Theatre Nuremberg)</li>
<li>Chairman of several foundations, e.g. the "Zukunftsstiftung der Sparkasse Nürnberg" (Future foundation of the Savings Bank Nuremberg)</li>
<li>Board of the "Congress- und Tourismuszentrale Nürnberg e. V." (Nuremberg Central Office of Congress and Tourism) and the "Deutsch-Amerikanisches Institut (DAI)" (German-American Institute) and other associations.</li>
</ul>
