<p><b>Nürnberg Falcons BC</b>, formerly called <b>Nürnberger BC</b> (abbreviated as <b>NBC</b>), for sponsorship reasons named <b>rent4office Nürnberg</b>, is a professional <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. The team plays in the <a href="/wiki/ProA" title="ProA">ProA</a>, the German professional second division. In 2010–11 the team promoted from the ProB to the ProA.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
