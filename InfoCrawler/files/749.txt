			<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">List of statistical offices in Germany</h1>
									<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
									<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
								<div id="contentSub"><span class="mw-redirectedfrom">  (Redirected from <a href="/w/index.php?title=Bayerisches_Landesamt_f%C3%BCr_Statistik_und_Datenverarbeitung&amp;redirect=no" class="mw-redirect" title="Bayerisches Landesamt für Statistik und Datenverarbeitung">Bayerisches Landesamt für Statistik und Datenverarbeitung</a>)</span></div>
												<div id="jump-to-nav" class="mw-jump">
					Jump to:					<a href="#mw-head">navigation</a>, 					<a href="#p-search">search</a>
				</div>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>statistical offices of the German states</b> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i><b>Statistische Landesämter</b></i>) carry out the task of collecting official <a href="/wiki/Statistics" title="Statistics">statistics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> together and in cooperation with the <b><a href="/wiki/Federal_Statistical_Office_of_Germany" title="Federal Statistical Office of Germany">Federal Statistical Office</a></b>.</p>
<p>The implementation of statistics according to Article 83 of the <a href="/wiki/Basic_Law_for_the_Federal_Republic_of_Germany" title="Basic Law for the Federal Republic of Germany">constitution</a> is executed at state level.<sup id="cite_ref-VIII_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-VIII-1">[1]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Bundestag" title="Bundestag">federal government</a> has, under Article 73 § 11 of the constitution, the exclusive <a href="/wiki/Legislation" title="Legislation">legislation</a> for the "statistics for federal purposes."<sup id="cite_ref-VII_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-VII-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<p>There are 14 statistical offices for the <a href="/wiki/States_of_Germany" title="States of Germany">16 states</a>:</p>
