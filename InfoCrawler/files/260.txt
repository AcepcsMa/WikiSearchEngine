											<div class="mw-parser-output"><p>PediaPress offers high-quality printed books based on wiki content and is an official partner of the <a class="external text" href="http://wikimediafoundation.org/">Wikimedia Foundation</a>.
</p><p>Clicking the <i>Order book from PediaPress</i> button, will take you to the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://pediapress.com/">website of PediaPress</a>. Once there you'll get a quote for your individual printed book and see a preview how it would look like.
