<p>Year <b>1580</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDLXXX</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Friday" title="Leap year starting on Friday">leap year starting on Friday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, and a <a href="/wiki/Leap_year_starting_on_Tuesday" title="Leap year starting on Tuesday">leap year starting on Tuesday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Proleptic_Gregorian_calendar" title="Proleptic Gregorian calendar">Proleptic Gregorian calendar</a>.</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1580&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_31" title="January 31">January 31</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry,_King_of_Portugal" title="Henry, King of Portugal">Henry, King of Portugal</a> dies with no direct heirs, precipitating a <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_succession_crisis_of_1580" title="Portuguese succession crisis of 1580">succession crisis</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_1" title="March 1">March 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/Michel_de_Montaigne" title="Michel de Montaigne">Michel de Montaigne</a> signs the preface to his most significant work, <i><a href="/wiki/Essays_(Montaigne)" title="Essays (Montaigne)">Essays</a></i>. They are published later this year.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_25" title="March 25">March 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Iberian_Union" title="Iberian Union">Iberian Union</a>: King <a href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_Spain" title="Philip II of Spain">Philip II of Spain</a> becomes King of Portugal under the name Philip I, following the death without heirs of King <a href="/wiki/Henry,_King_of_Portugal" title="Henry, King of Portugal">Henry of Portugal</a>, in a <a href="/wiki/Personal_union" title="Personal union">personal union</a> of the crowns, thus maintaining Portuguese independence (in Europe and throughout the <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_Empire" title="Portuguese Empire">Portuguese Empire</a>). The <a href="/wiki/Philippine_Dynasty" class="mw-redirect" title="Philippine Dynasty">Philippine Dynasty</a> rule lasts until <a href="/wiki/1640" title="1640">1640</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_6" title="April 6">April 6</a> – The <a href="/wiki/1580_Dover_Straits_earthquake" title="1580 Dover Straits earthquake">Dover Straits earthquake</a> occurs.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June" title="June">June</a> – <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a> signs a commercial treaty with the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Cassell.27s_Chronology_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Cassell.27s_Chronology-2">[2]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_11" title="June 11">June 11</a> – <a href="/wiki/Juan_de_Garay" title="Juan de Garay">Juan de Garay</a> founds <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">Buenos Aires</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_25" title="June 25">June 25</a> – The <i><a href="/wiki/Book_of_Concord" title="Book of Concord">Book of Concord</a></i>, a collection of <a href="/wiki/Lutheran" class="mw-redirect" title="Lutheran">Lutheran</a> confessional documents, is published.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1580&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_12" title="July 12">July 12</a> – The <i><a href="/wiki/Ostrog_Bible" title="Ostrog Bible">Ostrog Bible</a></i>, the first complete printed <a href="/wiki/Bible_translations_into_Slavic_languages" title="Bible translations into Slavic languages">Bible translation into a Slavic language</a> (<a href="/wiki/Old_Church_Slavonic" title="Old Church Slavonic">Old Church Slavonic</a>), is first printed at <a href="/wiki/Ostroh" title="Ostroh">Ostroh</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth</a> (modern-day <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>) by <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Fyodorov_(printer)" title="Ivan Fyodorov (printer)">Ivan Fyodorov</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_25" title="August 25">August 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Alc%C3%A2ntara_(1580)" title="Battle of Alcântara (1580)">Battle of Alcântara</a>: Spanish armies, led by <a href="/wiki/Fernando_%C3%81lvarez_de_Toledo,_3rd_Duke_of_Alba" title="Fernando Álvarez de Toledo, 3rd Duke of Alba">Fernando Álvarez de Toledo</a>, defending the claim of King <a href="/wiki/Philip_II_of_Spain" title="Philip II of Spain">Philip II of Spain</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_throne" class="mw-redirect" title="Portuguese throne">Portuguese throne</a>, defeat the armies of Portuguese claimant <a href="/wiki/Ant%C3%B3nio,_Prior_of_Crato" title="António, Prior of Crato">António, Prior of Crato</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_26" title="September 26">September 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Francis_Drake" title="Francis Drake">Francis Drake</a> returns to <a href="/wiki/Plymouth" title="Plymouth">Plymouth</a>, England from his voyage of <a href="/wiki/List_of_circumnavigations" title="List of circumnavigations">circumnavigation</a> (westabout) on the <i><a href="/wiki/Golden_Hind" title="Golden Hind">Golden Hind</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-Pocket_On_This_Day_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pocket_On_This_Day-3">[3]</a></sup> the second completed in a continuous voyage, and the first under its original commander.</li>
</ul>
