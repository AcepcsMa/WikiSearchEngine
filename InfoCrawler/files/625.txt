<p>The <b>Nuremberg S-Bahn</b> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <span lang="de" xml:lang="de"><i>S-Bahn Nürnberg</i></span>) is an <a href="/wiki/S-Bahn" class="mw-redirect" title="S-Bahn">S-Bahn</a> network covering the region of <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/F%C3%BCrth" title="Fürth">Fürth</a> and <a href="/wiki/Erlangen" title="Erlangen">Erlangen</a> which was founded in 1987 and is now integrated in the Transport Association Region Nuremberg (Verkehrsverbund Großraum Nürnberg). The full length of the four current lines is about 229 kilometres. The S-Bahn trains are operated by DB Regio Mittelfranken, a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Deutsche_Bahn" title="Deutsche Bahn">Deutsche Bahn</a> Regio Bayern; from December 2018 the service was due to be taken over by <a href="/wiki/National_Express_Germany" title="National Express Germany">National Express Germany</a>, however it withdrew from the bidding process in October 25 2016, the lines will continue to be operated by DB Regio Mittelfranken for the foreseeable future.</p>
