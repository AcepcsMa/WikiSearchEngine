<p>The <b>Kunsthalle Nürnberg</b> is an art centre founded in 1967, near the city centre. It organizes exhibitions by contemporary international artists in its galleries in <a href="/wiki/Nuremberg" title="Nuremberg">Nuremberg</a>. The Kunsthalle commissions new work by a majority of the artists it works with.</p>
