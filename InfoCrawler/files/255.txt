<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Wikipedia">Wikipedia</span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipedia_Signpost" title="Wikipedia:Wikipedia Signpost"><i>The Signpost</i></a> (Shortcut: <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:POST" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:POST">WP:POST</a>, <a class="external text" href="http://tools.wmflabs.org/mediawiki-feeds/feed.php?url=https%3A%2F%2Fen.wikipedia.org%2Fw%2F&amp;category=Category%3AWikipedia+Signpost+RSS+feed&amp;title=The+Signpost">RSS</a>). The Wikipedia community newspaper that is written by volunteers and published weekly.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Milestones" title="Wikipedia:Milestones">Wikipedia:Milestones</a> (Shortcut: <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:ANN" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikipedia:ANN">WP:ANN</a>, <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://jeays.net/wikipedia/announcements.xml">RSS</a>).
