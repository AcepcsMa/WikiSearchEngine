<p><b>September 26</b> is the 269th day of the year (270th in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 96 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Monday, Wednesday or Saturday (58 in 400 years each) than on Thursday or Friday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Tuesday or Sunday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=September_26&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/46_BC" title="46 BC">46 BC</a> – <a href="/wiki/Julius_Caesar" title="Julius Caesar">Julius Caesar</a> dedicates a <a href="/wiki/Temple" title="Temple">temple</a> to his mythical ancestor <a href="/wiki/Venus_(mythology)" title="Venus (mythology)">Venus Genetrix</a> in accordance with a vow he made at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Pharsalus" title="Battle of Pharsalus">Battle of Pharsalus</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/715" title="715">715</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ragenfrid" title="Ragenfrid">Ragenfrid</a> defeats <a href="/wiki/Theudoald" title="Theudoald">Theudoald</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Compi%C3%A8gne" title="Battle of Compiègne">Battle of Compiègne</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1087" title="1087">1087</a> – <a href="/wiki/William_II_of_England" title="William II of England">William II</a> is crowned <a href="/wiki/King_of_England" class="mw-redirect" title="King of England">King of England</a>, and reigns until 1100.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1212" title="1212">1212</a> – <a href="/wiki/Golden_Bull_of_Sicily" title="Golden Bull of Sicily">Golden Bull of Sicily</a> is issued to confirm the hereditary royal title in <a href="/wiki/Bohemia" title="Bohemia">Bohemia</a> for the <a href="/wiki/P%C5%99emyslid_dynasty" title="Přemyslid dynasty">Přemyslid dynasty</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1345" title="1345">1345</a> – <a href="/wiki/Friso-Hollandic_Wars" title="Friso-Hollandic Wars">Friso-Hollandic Wars</a>: <a href="/wiki/Frisians" title="Frisians">Frisians</a> defeat <a href="/wiki/Holland" title="Holland">Holland</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Warns" title="Battle of Warns">Battle of Warns</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1371" title="1371">1371</a> – <a href="/wiki/History_of_the_Serbian%E2%80%93Turkish_wars" class="mw-redirect" title="History of the Serbian–Turkish wars">Serbian–Turkish wars</a>: The forces of the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman</a> sultan <a href="/wiki/Murad_I" title="Murad I">Murad I</a>'s lieutenant <a href="/wiki/Lala_%C5%9Eahin_Pasha" title="Lala Şahin Pasha">Lala Şahin Pasha</a> and the Serbian army under the command of <a href="/wiki/Vuka%C5%A1in_Mrnjav%C4%8Devi%C4%87" class="mw-redirect" title="Vukašin Mrnjavčević">Vukašin Mrnjavčević</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jovan_Uglje%C5%A1a" class="mw-redirect" title="Jovan Uglješa">Jovan Uglješa</a> clash at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Maritsa" title="Battle of Maritsa">Battle of Maritsa</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1493" title="1493">1493</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Alexander_VI" title="Pope Alexander VI">Pope Alexander VI</a> issues the <a href="/wiki/Papal_bull" title="Papal bull">papal bull</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Dudum_siquidem" title="Dudum siquidem">Dudum siquidem</a></i> to the <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Monarchs" title="Catholic Monarchs">Catholic Monarchs</a>, extending the grant of new lands he made them in <i><a href="/wiki/Inter_caetera" title="Inter caetera">Inter caetera</a></i>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1580" title="1580">1580</a> – <a href="/wiki/Sir" title="Sir">Sir</a> <a href="/wiki/Francis_Drake" title="Francis Drake">Francis Drake</a> finishes his circumnavigation of the Earth.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1687" title="1687">1687</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Parthenon" title="Parthenon">Parthenon</a> in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a> is partially destroyed by an explosion caused by the bombing from <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Venice" title="Republic of Venice">Venetian forces</a> led by <a href="/wiki/Francesco_Morosini" title="Francesco Morosini">Morosini</a> who are besieging the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Turks" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottoman Turks">Ottoman Turks</a> stationed in <a href="/wiki/Athens" title="Athens">Athens</a>.</li>
<li>1687 – The city council of <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a> votes to support <a href="/wiki/William_III_of_England" title="William III of England">William of Orange</a>'s invasion of England, which became the <a href="/wiki/Glorious_Revolution" title="Glorious Revolution">Glorious Revolution</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1777" title="1777">1777</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolution" title="American Revolution">American Revolution</a>: British troops <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_campaign" title="Philadelphia campaign">occupy</a> <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1789" title="1789">1789</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Jefferson" title="Thomas Jefferson">Thomas Jefferson</a> is appointed the first <a href="/wiki/United_States_Secretary_of_State" title="United States Secretary of State">United States Secretary of State</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Jay" title="John Jay">John Jay</a> is appointed the first <a href="/wiki/United_States_Supreme_Court/Chief_Justice" class="mw-redirect" title="United States Supreme Court/Chief Justice">Chief Justice of the United States</a>, <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Osgood" title="Samuel Osgood">Samuel Osgood</a> is appointed the first <a href="/wiki/United_States_Postmaster_General" title="United States Postmaster General">United States Postmaster General</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Edmund_Randolph" title="Edmund Randolph">Edmund Randolph</a> is appointed the first <a href="/wiki/United_States_Attorney_General" title="United States Attorney General">United States Attorney General</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1792" title="1792">1792</a> – <a href="/wiki/Marc_David_Alba_Lasource" title="Marc David Alba Lasource">Marc-David Lasource</a> begins accusing <a href="/wiki/Maximilien_Robespierre" title="Maximilien Robespierre">Maximilien Robespierre</a> of wanting a <a href="/wiki/Dictatorship" title="Dictatorship">dictatorship</a> for France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1799" title="1799">1799</a> – War of the 2nd Coalition: <a href="/wiki/Second_Battle_of_Zurich" title="Second Battle of Zurich">Second Battle of Zurich</a> Austro-Russian forces under <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Korsakov" title="Alexander Korsakov">Korsakov</a> are defeated by Franco-Swiss under <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Mass%C3%A9na" title="André Masséna">André Masséna</a>, leading to the collapse of <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Suvorov" title="Alexander Suvorov">Alexander Suvorov</a>'s campaign.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1810" title="1810">1810</a> – A new <a href="/wiki/Swedish_Act_of_Succession" title="Swedish Act of Succession">Act of Succession</a> is adopted by the <a href="/wiki/Riksdag_of_the_Estates" title="Riksdag of the Estates">Riksdag of the Estates</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_XIV_of_Sweden" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles XIV of Sweden">Jean Baptiste Bernadotte</a> becomes heir to the Swedish throne.</li>
