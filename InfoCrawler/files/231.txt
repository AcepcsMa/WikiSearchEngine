<p><b>September 27</b> is the 270th day of the year (271st in <a href="/wiki/Leap_year" title="Leap year">leap years</a>) in the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>. There are 95 days remaining until the end of the year. This date is slightly more likely to fall on a Tuesday, Thursday or Sunday (58 in 400 years each) than on Friday or Saturday (57), and slightly less likely to occur on a Monday or Wednesday (56).</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=September_27&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/1066" title="1066">1066</a> – <a href="/wiki/William_the_Conqueror" title="William the Conqueror">William the Conqueror</a> and his army set sail from the mouth of the <a href="/wiki/River_Somme" class="mw-redirect" title="River Somme">River Somme</a>, beginning the <a href="/wiki/Norman_conquest_of_England" title="Norman conquest of England">Norman conquest of England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1331" title="1331">1331</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_P%C5%82owce" title="Battle of Płowce">Battle of Płowce</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Poland_(1025%E2%80%931385)" title="Kingdom of Poland (1025–1385)">Kingdom of Poland</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Teutonic_Order" title="Teutonic Order">Teutonic Order</a> is fought.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1422" title="1422">1422</a> – After the brief <a href="/wiki/Gollub_War" title="Gollub War">Gollub War</a> the <a href="/wiki/Teutonic_Knights" class="mw-redirect" title="Teutonic Knights">Teutonic Knights</a> sign the <a href="/wiki/Treaty_of_Melno" title="Treaty of Melno">Treaty of Melno</a> with the <a href="/wiki/History_of_Poland_during_the_Jagiellonian_dynasty" title="History of Poland during the Jagiellonian dynasty">Kingdom of Poland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Lithuania" title="Grand Duchy of Lithuania">Grand Duchy of Lithuania</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1529" title="1529">1529</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Vienna" title="Siege of Vienna">Siege of Vienna</a> begins when <a href="/wiki/Suleiman_the_Magnificent" title="Suleiman the Magnificent">Suleiman I</a> attacks the city.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1540" title="1540">1540</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Society_of_Jesus" title="Society of Jesus">Society of Jesus</a> (Jesuits) receives its <a href="/wiki/Charter" title="Charter">charter</a> from <a href="/wiki/Pope_Paul_III" title="Pope Paul III">Pope Paul III</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1590" title="1590">1590</a> – <a href="/wiki/Pope_Urban_VII" title="Pope Urban VII">Pope Urban VII</a> dies 13 days after being chosen as the Pope, making his reign <a href="/wiki/List_of_popes_by_length_of_reign" class="mw-redirect" title="List of popes by length of reign">the shortest papacy in history</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1605" title="1605">1605</a> – The armies of Sweden are defeated by the <a href="/wiki/Polish%E2%80%93Lithuanian_Commonwealth" title="Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth">Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Kircholm" class="mw-redirect" title="Battle of Kircholm">Battle of Kircholm</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1669" title="1669">1669</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Venice" title="Republic of Venice">Venetians</a> surrender the fortress of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Candia" title="Kingdom of Candia">Candia</a> to the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottomans</a>, thus ending the 21-year-long <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Candia" title="Siege of Candia">Siege of Candia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1777" title="1777">1777</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: <a href="/wiki/Lancaster,_Pennsylvania" title="Lancaster, Pennsylvania">Lancaster, Pennsylvania</a> becomes the capital of the United States, for one day after the <a href="/wiki/Second_Continental_Congress" title="Second Continental Congress">Second Continental Congress</a> evacuates <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia" title="Philadelphia">Philadelphia</a> to avoid invading British forces.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1822" title="1822">1822</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jean-Fran%C3%A7ois_Champollion" title="Jean-François Champollion">Jean-François Champollion</a> announces that he has deciphered the <a href="/wiki/Rosetta_Stone" title="Rosetta Stone">Rosetta Stone</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1825" title="1825">1825</a> – The world's first public railway to use steam locomotives, the <a href="/wiki/Stockton_and_Darlington_Railway" title="Stockton and Darlington Railway">Stockton and Darlington Railway</a>, is ceremonially opened.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1854" title="1854">1854</a> – The steamship <a href="/wiki/SS_Arctic" title="SS Arctic">SS&#160;<i>Arctic</i></a> <a href="/wiki/SS_Arctic_disaster" title="SS Arctic disaster">sinks</a> with 300 people on board. This marks the first great disaster in the Atlantic Ocean.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1875" title="1875">1875</a> – The merchant sailing ship <i><a href="/wiki/Ellen_Southard" title="Ellen Southard">Ellen Southard</a></i> is wrecked in a storm at <a href="/wiki/Liverpool" title="Liverpool">Liverpool</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1903" title="1903">1903</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Wreck_of_the_Old_97" title="Wreck of the Old 97">Wreck of the Old 97</a>, an American rail disaster that became the subject of a popular ballad.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1905" title="1905">1905</a> – The physics journal <i><a href="/wiki/Annalen_der_Physik" title="Annalen der Physik">Annalen der Physik</a></i> received <a href="/wiki/Albert_Einstein" title="Albert Einstein">Albert Einstein</a>'s paper, "Does the Inertia of a Body Depend Upon Its Energy Content?", introducing the equation <a href="/wiki/E%3Dmc%C2%B2" class="mw-redirect" title="E=mc²">E=mc²</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a> – The first production of the <a href="/wiki/Ford_Model_T" title="Ford Model T">Ford Model T</a> automobile was built at the <a href="/wiki/Ford_Piquette_Avenue_Plant" title="Ford Piquette Avenue Plant">Ford Piquette Avenue Plant</a> in <a href="/wiki/Detroit" title="Detroit">Detroit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michigan" title="Michigan">Michigan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1916" title="1916">1916</a> – <a href="/wiki/Iyasu_V" title="Iyasu V">Iyasu V</a> is proclaimed deposed as ruler of <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a> in a palace coup in favor of his aunt <a href="/wiki/Zewditu" title="Zewditu">Zewditu</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1922" title="1922">1922</a> – King <a href="/wiki/Constantine_I_of_Greece" title="Constantine I of Greece">Constantine I of Greece</a> abdicates his throne in favor of his eldest son, <a href="/wiki/George_II_of_Greece" title="George II of Greece">George II</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1928" title="1928">1928</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Republic_of_China_(1912%E2%80%9349)" title="Republic of China (1912–49)">Republic of China</a> is recognized by the United States.</li>
