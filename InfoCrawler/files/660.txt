<p>The <b>ProA</b> is the <a href="/wiki/German_basketball_league_system" title="German basketball league system">second-tier level</a> <a href="/wiki/Sports_league" title="Sports league">league</a> of professional club <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. The league comprises 16 teams. Officially the ProA is part of the <i><a href="/wiki/2._Basketball_Bundesliga" title="2. Basketball Bundesliga">2. Basketball Bundesliga</a></i>, which consists of the two hierarchical leagues <i>ProA</i> and <i>ProB</i>. Before the 2007–08 season, the <i>2. Basketball Bundesliga</i> was a basketball league with the same name, which consisted of two geographical divisions. At the end of the league stage, the top two teams qualify for the <a href="/wiki/Basketball_Bundesliga" title="Basketball Bundesliga">Basketball Bundesliga</a>, and the teams positioned 15th and 16th are <a href="/wiki/Promotion_and_relegation" title="Promotion and relegation">relegated</a> to the lower league, <a href="/wiki/ProB" title="ProB">ProB</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
