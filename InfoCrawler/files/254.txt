<p>The <b>Wikipedia reference desk</b> works like a library <a href="/wiki/Reference_desk" title="Reference desk">reference desk</a>. Ask a question here and Wikipedia volunteers will try to answer it.</p>
<p>Before asking a question, please try <b>the search boxes at right</b> to search Wikipedia as a whole or the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reference_desk/Archives" title="Wikipedia:Reference desk/Archives">Reference Desk archives</a>. See also the <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reference_desk/Guidelines" title="Wikipedia:Reference desk/Guidelines">Reference Desk guidelines</a>.</p>
<p><b>Note:</b> Legal or medical advice is prohibited. Further information is available at Wikipedia's <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Legal_disclaimer" title="Wikipedia:Legal disclaimer">legal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Medical_disclaimer" title="Wikipedia:Medical disclaimer">medical</a> disclaimers. See also <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reference_desk/Guidelines/Medical_advice" title="Wikipedia:Reference desk/Guidelines/Medical advice">Medical advice guidelines</a>.</p>
